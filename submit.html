Comprehensive Exam Application

Fill out this form to apply for your comprehensive examination. This replaces the paper form TSU-VPA-SF-39. Make sure you have already paid at the Cashiering Office before submitting.

What you need ready

  • Your O.R. number & date of payment
  • List of subjects taken (Basic, Major, Electives)
  • Preferred date of exam
  • Active Gmail address

Use this form to submit your current step in the oral defense process. The office will update your status and notify you of the next steps by email.

What you need ready

  • Your thesis / dissertation title
  • Name of preferred adviser
  • Current step you are on (Steps 1–8)
  • O.R. number (if already paid)

General Inquiry

Send your questions, follow-ups, or concerns to the Graduate School office. No more chasing staff on personal Messenger — all inquiries go through here.

What you need ready

  • Your full name
  • Type of concern
  • Clear description of your question
  • Active Gmail address for reply

Response time: The office aims to respond within 1–3 working days in accordance with RA 11032 – Ease of Doing Business Act.
